About Me

Hello, I am Gökberk Söğüt, an Electrical and Electronics Engineer with significant experience in both the energy sector and different industries. My primary focus areas are hardware engineering, power rooms and power transmission, where I have excelled throughout my career. Leveraging my expertise, I have actively contributed to key projects and demonstrated a consistent track record of successful completion.

As a seasoned professional with a diverse background, I have successfully navigated key roles at industry leaders such as Huawei, Lectra, Eigen, and Egemsoft. In my capacity as a Production Engineer at Huawei, I demonstrated prowess in managing and enhancing 4G and 5G telecommunication equipment production processes. My responsibilities included coordinating maintenance and repairs and conducting comprehensive data analysis for error detection and correction, showcasing my dedication to optimizing operational efficiency.

With a strong foundation in both hardware and software engineering, I am dedicated to leveraging my skills to drive innovation and excellence in the field of modern engineering. My experience spans across various industries, including telecommunications, aerospace, and software development, underscoring my versatility and adaptability in tackling complex challenges.
